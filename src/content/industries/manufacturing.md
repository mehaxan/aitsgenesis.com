## Manufacturing in the Age of Industry 4.0

The factory of the future is connected, data-driven, and increasingly autonomous. Digital twins, real-time production monitoring, predictive maintenance, and automated quality control are no longer future concepts — they are operational realities for competitive manufacturers.

## Manufacturing Technology Solutions

**Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES)**
Real-time production tracking, work order management, shop floor scheduling, material consumption tracking, and OEE measurement that connects plant operations to business planning.

**Predictive Maintenance**
IoT sensor data combined with machine learning models that predict equipment failures before they cause unplanned downtime — dramatically reducing maintenance costs and production disruption.

**Quality Management Systems**
Digital quality workflows, statistical process control (SPC), non-conformance management, corrective action tracking, and audit management for ISO and industry-specific standards.

**Production Planning & Scheduling**
Advanced planning and scheduling (APS) systems that optimise production sequences, resource allocation, and material availability across complex manufacturing operations.

**Industrial IoT Integration**
Connecting PLCs, SCADA systems, and industrial equipment to data platforms for real-time visibility, anomaly detection, and process optimisation.

**ERP Integration**
Seamless integration between MES, quality, and maintenance systems with SAP, Oracle, and other ERP platforms for unified operational and financial visibility.

**Digital Twin**
Virtual replicas of production lines and equipment that enable simulation, optimisation, and what-if analysis before implementing changes on the physical plant floor.
