## Building the Financial Infrastructure of Tomorrow

Fintech moves fast — regulatory landscapes shift, user expectations evolve, and competitive dynamics compress timelines. We deliver fintech products at startup velocity without sacrificing the security and compliance that financial services demand.

## Fintech Products We Build

**Payment Platforms**
Payment processing, gateway integration (Stripe, Adyen, Razorpay), payment orchestration, recurring billing, and cross-border payment solutions with multi-currency support.

**Digital Wallets**
E-money platforms with card issuance, peer-to-peer transfers, merchant payments, and loyalty programs. Compliant with EMI regulatory requirements.

**Neobank & Challenger Bank Infrastructure**
Current accounts, savings products, debit cards, and overdraft facilities — built on modern BaaS platforms with a beautiful, opinionated mobile UX.

**Embedded Finance**
Financial products embedded in non-financial platforms — BNPL in e-commerce, lending in B2B SaaS, insurance in mobility apps. We build the APIs and compliance rails.

**Investment & Wealth Management**
Retail investment platforms, robo-advisory engines, portfolio management tools, and investment product distribution infrastructure.

**Crypto & Digital Assets**
Custody solutions, exchange infrastructure, DeFi protocol development, and regulated crypto product development with compliance overlays.

## Regulatory Expertise

We understand the fintech regulatory environment — FCA authorisation, PSD2, AML/KYC, GDPR, and emerging crypto regulation. We build compliance into the product architecture from the start, not bolted on as an afterthought.
