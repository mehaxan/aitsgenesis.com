## Real Estate Runs on Relationships and Data — We Digitise Both

The real estate industry manages complex, high-value transactions involving multiple parties, regulatory requirements, and long timelines. Technology that organises this complexity creates competitive advantage for agents, agencies, developers, and property managers.

## Real Estate Technology Solutions

**Property Management Systems**
End-to-end property management for landlords and management companies — tenant management, lease tracking, maintenance requests, rent collection, and financial reporting.

**Agency CRM & Lead Management**
Sales pipeline management, lead capture and nurturing, viewings management, offer tracking, and performance reporting for real estate agencies and brokers.

**Property Listing Portals**
Search portals with advanced filtering, map-based search, virtual tours, mortgage calculators, and agent contact management for residential and commercial properties.

**Tenant & Landlord Portals**
Self-service portals for tenants to pay rent, log maintenance requests, and access documents — and for landlords to manage their portfolios with full visibility.

**Digital Transaction Management**
Secure document management, e-signature integration, compliance checklist tracking, and completion management for property transactions.

**Property Analytics**
Market value analytics, rental yield benchmarking, portfolio performance reporting, and investment decision support for property investors and developers.
