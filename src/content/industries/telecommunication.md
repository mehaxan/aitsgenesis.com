## Building Modern Telecom Experiences on Legacy Foundations

Telecom operators face the challenge of delivering consumer-grade digital experiences on infrastructure built over decades. We specialise in the integration and modernisation work that bridges old and new — unlocking digital value without disrupting operational continuity.

## Telecom Technology Solutions

**BSS/OSS Modernisation**
Evolutionary migration of billing, mediation, provisioning, and network management systems to modern micro-service architectures that enable agility without big-bang replacement risk.

**Self-service Portals & Apps**
Digital customer portals and mobile apps for account management, plan changes, bill payment, usage monitoring, and service requests — reducing contact centre volume and cost.

**Network Analytics**
Quality of service monitoring, network performance dashboards, capacity planning analytics, and predictive fault detection across fixed and mobile network elements.

**Revenue Assurance & Fraud Management**
Automated detection of billing leakage, usage anomalies, and fraudulent subscription activity — protecting revenue and margins.

**MVNO Platforms**
End-to-end MVNO enablement — subscriber management, billing, provisioning, and customer self-service for operators launching virtual network services.

**Telecom API Marketplace**
Developer portals and API management platforms that monetise network capabilities — SMS, voice, number verification, and edge compute APIs — to enterprise and developer customers.

**Field Workforce Management**
Digital tools for field technicians — job dispatch, navigation, digital job cards, inventory management, and customer sign-off workflows.
