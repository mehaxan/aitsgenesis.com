## Technology at the Root of Agricultural Transformation

Agriculture must produce 70% more food by 2050 with less land, less water, and a changing climate. Technology — from satellite imagery and IoT soil sensors to AI yield models and supply chain platforms — is the primary path to achieving that.

## Agritech Solutions

**Farm Management Information Systems**
Digital farm records, crop planning, input tracking, labour management, equipment logs, and compliance reporting for farms both small and enterprise-scale.

**Precision Agriculture**
IoT sensor networks, drone data integration, variable rate application, and data-driven field intervention — optimising inputs (fertiliser, water, pesticide) across field variation.

**Crop Monitoring & Remote Sensing**
Satellite and drone imagery analysis for crop health monitoring, disease detection, yield estimation, and irrigation scheduling.

**Agricultural Supply Chain Traceability**
Farm-to-fork traceability platforms that record provenance, handling conditions, and certifications across the supply chain — meeting retailer and regulatory requirements.

**Agri Marketplace Platforms**
Digital platforms connecting farmers directly to buyers, input suppliers, and service providers — reducing middlemen, improving price discovery, and increasing market access.

**Microfinance & Agri Finance**
Credit scoring models for smallholder farmers, digital loan disbursement, and repayment tracking tools that expand access to agricultural finance.

**Weather & Crop Insurance**
Index-based insurance products with parametric triggers, satellite verification, and automated claims settlement for climate risk protection.
