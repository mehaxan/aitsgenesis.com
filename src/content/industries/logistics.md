## Logistics Complexity Solved Through Technology

The modern supply chain spans continents, involves dozens of parties, and must operate with near-zero tolerance for error or delay. Technology is the only viable solution to the coordination and visibility challenges this complexity creates.

## Logistics Technology Solutions

**Transport Management Systems**
End-to-end TMS covering shipment planning, carrier allocation, freight booking, track-and-trace, and freight audit — for both asset-based carriers and 3PL operators.

**Fleet Tracking & Telematics**
Real-time GPS tracking, driver behaviour monitoring, fuel consumption analytics, predictive maintenance alerts, and electronic logging for compliance.

**Warehouse Management Systems**
Optimised picking, packing, and put-away workflows, inventory accuracy tools, labour management, and integration with order management and ERP systems.

**Route Optimisation**
AI-powered routing that minimises distance, time, and fuel cost while respecting vehicle capacity, time windows, driver hours regulations, and traffic conditions.

**Last-mile Delivery Platforms**
Consumer-facing tracking, flexible delivery windows, proof of delivery capture, failed delivery management, and carrier integration for the final customer touchpoint.

**Supply Chain Visibility**
Multi-tier supply chain tracking that provides real-time visibility from supplier to customer — enabling proactive exception management and accurate ETA prediction.

**Freight Marketplace & Carrier Matching**
Digital platforms that match freight with capacity, enabling real-time bidding, spot rate procurement, and carrier performance management.
