## Mobility Technology for the Future of Getting Around

Transportation is undergoing profound transformation — electric vehicles, ride-sharing, autonomous systems, and multimodal MaaS platforms are reshaping how cities and supply chains move. Software is at the centre of every one of these shifts.

## Transportation Solutions

**Ride-hailing & Dispatch Platforms**
Real-time passenger dispatch, driver management, dynamic pricing, payment processing, and ratings systems for taxi companies, ride-hailing platforms, and corporate mobility providers.

**Fleet Management Systems**
Real-time vehicle tracking, driver behaviour monitoring, fuel management, preventive maintenance scheduling, compliance management, and utilisation analytics for commercial fleets.

**Public Transport Technology**
Contactless ticketing, real-time passenger information displays, journey planning integrations, capacity management, and operations analytics for public transport operators.

**Mobility-as-a-Service (MaaS)**
Integrated mobility platforms that combine public transport, ride-hailing, bike-sharing, and micro-mobility into a single journey planning and payment platform.

**Freight & Cargo Management**
Freight booking, shipment tracking, driver app, proof of delivery, and carrier performance management for road freight operators.

**Vehicle Telematics**
GPS tracking, driver scoring, fuel analytics, geofencing, and remote diagnostics integrated with fleet management and insurance systems.

**Autonomous Vehicle Support Systems**
Data platforms, simulation environments, and operational support systems for companies developing and deploying autonomous vehicle technology.
