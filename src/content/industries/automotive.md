## The Automotive Industry Is Becoming a Software Industry

Modern vehicles are as much software as they are hardware. Connectivity, electrification, autonomous features, and digital retail experiences are creating new software requirements throughout the automotive value chain — from OEM to dealer to driver.

## Automotive Technology Solutions

**Connected Car Platforms**
In-vehicle app ecosystems, over-the-air update (OTA) infrastructure, telematics platforms, remote diagnostics, and connected services apps for OEMs and fleet operators.

**Dealer Management Systems**
Integrated DMS for vehicle inventory, sales pipeline, F&I workflow, service scheduling, parts management, and financial reporting for franchise and independent dealers.

**Vehicle Configurator & E-commerce**
Online vehicle configurators, direct-to-consumer sales platforms, trade-in valuation tools, finance calculators, and homedelivery journey management.

**After-sales & Service Platforms**
Service booking, digital job cards, technician workshop management, warranty claims, parts ordering, and customer communication for OEM and aftermarket service.

**Fleet & Leasing Management**
Contract lifecycle management, fleet composition optimisation, maintenance scheduling, driver management, and cost analytics for fleet operators and leasing companies.

**EV Infrastructure Software**
EV charging network management, smart charging algorithms, billing and payment, energy management, and driver apps for charging network operators.

**Automotive Data Analytics**
Connected vehicle data analytics, after-sales analytics, dealership performance benchmarking, and customer lifecycle analytics for marketing and service operations.
