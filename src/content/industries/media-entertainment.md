## Technology at the Heart of Modern Media

From independent content creators to global entertainment conglomerates, the ability to distribute, monetise, and build community around content is now entirely software-dependent. The platforms that audiences love are built on thoughtful engineering.

## Media & Entertainment Solutions

**OTT Streaming Platforms**
End-to-end video streaming infrastructure — content ingestion, transcoding, adaptive bitrate delivery, DRM, subscriber management, and cross-device apps for smart TV, web, iOS, and Android.

**Content Management Systems**
Media-specific CMS built for publishing teams — structured content modelling, editorial workflow, multi-language support, and headless API delivery to any channel.

**Fan Engagement Platforms**
Dedicated community apps for sports teams, music artists, and entertainment brands — exclusive content, live chat, polls, merchandise integration, and event ticketing.

**Digital Publishing**
Magazine and newspaper digital platforms — article management, subscription paywalls, push notifications, personalised feeds, and advertising integration.

**Live Streaming & Events**
Real-time streaming infrastructure for sports, concerts, conferences, and interactive events — with chat, polls, viewer monetisation, and CDN delivery at scale.

**Audience Analytics**
Viewer behaviour analysis, content performance reporting, churn prediction, recommendation algorithm data, and advertising effectiveness measurement.

**Subscription & Monetisation**
Flexible subscription tiers, pay-per-view, ad-supported and hybrid monetisation models with payment handling, dunning, and revenue analytics.
