## Travel Technology That Serves the Modern Traveller

Today's traveller expects instant booking, personalised experiences, frictionless check-in, and real-time service. The technology behind seamless travel and hospitality experiences is complex — and increasingly the primary differentiator between brands.

## Travel & Hospitality Solutions

**Online Booking Engines**
Custom booking engines for hotels, tours, activities, and transport — with availability management, dynamic pricing, payment processing, and confirmation workflows.

**Property Management Systems**
Comprehensive hotel PMS covering reservations, front desk operations, housekeeping management, maintenance, and financial accounting.

**OTA & Distribution Platforms**
Online travel agency (OTA) platforms or distribution channel managers that connect inventory to booking channels, GDS networks, and direct booking sites.

**Guest Experience Applications**
Mobile apps for pre-arrival communication, digital check-in/out, in-stay requests, F&B ordering, concierge services, and post-stay feedback.

**Revenue Management**
Dynamic pricing engines that optimise rate strategies based on demand signals, competitor pricing, booking window, and market conditions.

**Tour & Activity Platforms**
Booking, scheduling, guide management, and customer communication systems for tour operators, activity providers, and experience companies.

**Loyalty & CRM**
Points programmes, tier management, partner rewards, and CRM systems that drive repeat bookings and personalised marketing for travel brands.
