## Machine Learning That Generates Measurable Business Value

ML is only valuable when it's deployed, adopted, and maintained. We go beyond model building — we engineer the full lifecycle from data preparation and model training to production serving, monitoring, and continuous improvement.

## ML Capabilities We Deliver

**Classification & Regression**
Predictive models for credit scoring, churn prediction, lead conversion likelihood, pricing optimisation, and outcome forecasting.

**Natural Language Processing**
Text classification, sentiment analysis, named entity recognition, document summarisation, semantic search, and chatbot intelligence.

**Computer Vision**
Image classification, object detection and tracking, optical character recognition, quality inspection in manufacturing, and medical image analysis.

**Recommendation Systems**
Collaborative filtering, content-based filtering, and hybrid recommenders that increase engagement and basket size in e-commerce and media platforms.

**Anomaly Detection**
Real-time detection of fraudulent transactions, network intrusions, equipment failures, and data quality issues.

**Time Series Forecasting**
Demand forecasting, energy load prediction, financial market modelling, and operational capacity planning.

## MLOps: Keeping Models Working in Production

A model in a notebook solves nothing. We build the infrastructure to serve predictions at low latency, monitor for model degradation and data drift, automate retraining pipelines, and maintain model version registries — so your ML investment keeps delivering value over time.
