## When Simulated Experience Creates Real Learning and Engagement

Virtual reality creates conditions that are impossible, dangerous, or prohibitively expensive in the physical world. A trainee can practice a surgical procedure, respond to a factory emergency, or navigate a complex negotiation — safely, repeatedly, and measurably.

## VR Experiences We Create

**Enterprise Training Simulations**
Safety training, equipment operation, emergency procedures, customer service scenarios, and soft skills practice — all in safe, repeatable, measurable VR environments that transfer learning more effectively than classroom or video training.

**Healthcare & Medical VR**
Surgical simulation, patient rehabilitation, exposure therapy for phobias and PTSD, pain management applications, and medical education.

**Architecture & Real Estate Visualisation**
Walk through buildings that don't yet exist. Let clients experience spaces at human scale, explore material choices, and approve designs with confidence before construction begins.

**Virtual Showrooms**
Replace physical showrooms with virtual environments that can be updated instantly, accessed globally, and experienced by customers from their homes.

**Product Design & Engineering**
Collaborative VR environments for engineering review, ergonomics testing, and design sign-off — enabling distributed teams to evaluate physical products before manufacture.

**Multi-user VR Collaboration**
Shared virtual workspaces for remote teams — whiteboarding, 3D model review, spatial collaboration that genuinely improves on 2D video conferencing.

## Platform Expertise

We develop for Meta Quest (standalone), PC VR headsets via SteamVR/OpenXR, mobile VR (Google Cardboard, Gear VR), and WebXR for browser-based experiences requiring no headset install.
