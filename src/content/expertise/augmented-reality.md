## AR Makes the Digital World Tangible

Augmented reality adds a dimension to human-computer interaction that conventional screens cannot match. It places digital information exactly where it's most useful — in the context of the physical world.

## AR Applications We Build

**Retail & E-commerce AR**
"See it in your room" furniture shopping, "try before you buy" fashion and accessories, and product configurators that let customers see exactly what they're ordering before purchase.

**Industrial & Manufacturing AR**
Step-by-step assembly guidance overlaid on physical equipment, maintenance instructions displayed on machines, quality inspection guidance, and remote expert assistance.

**Training & Education**
Contextual training where learners interact with overlaid information on real environments — anatomy study, equipment training, safety procedure practice.

**Architecture & Real Estate**
Overlay architectural plans on building sites, let prospective buyers see finished interiors in empty spaces, or visualise planned renovations in existing rooms.

**Marketing & Brand Experiences**
AR-powered packaging, interactive marketing campaigns, branded filters, and memorable experiences that drive social sharing and brand recall.

**WebAR (No App Required)**
Browser-based AR accessible to anyone with a smartphone camera — no App Store friction. Ideal for marketing campaigns and one-time consumer experiences.

## 3D Asset Production

Great AR requires great 3D assets. Our team produces optimised, photorealistic, or stylised 3D models, textures, and animations tailored for mobile AR performance.
