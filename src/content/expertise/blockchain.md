## Blockchain Excellence Across Public and Private Networks

From DeFi protocols on Ethereum to consortium chains for supply chain transparency, our blockchain engineers have delivered production-grade distributed ledger solutions across the full spectrum of use cases and platforms.

**Ethereum & EVM-compatible Chains**

**Hyperledger Fabric**
Enterprise-grade permissioned blockchain for industries where privacy, regulatory compliance, and performance requirements rule out public chains.

**Solana**

**Polygon & Layer 2**
Scalable solutions on Ethereum's Layer 2 ecosystem for applications that need Ethereum's security without its cost and speed constraints.

**Cross-chain Solutions**
Bridges and interoperability solutions that enable asset and data movement across blockchain networks.

## Smart Contract Quality

Security vulnerabilities in smart contracts are irreversible on-chain. Our development process includes:
- Formal specification before coding
- Test coverage on all execution paths
- Slither and Mythril static analysis
- Manual security review by senior engineers
- Independent audit coordination

## When to Choose Public vs. Private Blockchain

We help you make the right architectural choice — public chains for trustless, censorship-resistant, globally accessible applications; permissioned chains for consortium data sharing where identity, privacy, and performance requirements demand it.
