## When Physical and Digital Intelligence Converge

Billions of physical assets — machines, vehicles, buildings, products, environmental sensors — now generate valuable data. The companies that connect, collect, and act on that data gain operational intelligence that was previously impossible.

## Our IoT Services

**IoT Platform Design**
Architecture for scalable IoT systems — device management, data ingestion at scale, storage, processing, and delivery to applications and dashboards.

**Device & Firmware Development**
Embedded software for microcontrollers and edge devices — firmware development, protocol implementation, power optimisation, and OTA update frameworks.

**Edge Computing**
Move intelligence closer to the data source with edge computing. Reduce latency, bandwidth costs, and cloud dependency with local processing and intelligent pre-filtering.

**Industrial IoT (IIoT)**
Connected factory solutions — machine monitoring, predictive maintenance, production line telemetry, and integration with existing manufacturing execution systems (MES/ERP).

**Smart Building & Facilities**
BMS integration, energy monitoring, environmental sensors, predictive HVAC maintenance, and occupancy analytics.

**Asset Tracking & Fleet Management**
GPS and sensor-based tracking solutions with real-time visibility into asset location, condition, utilisation, and maintenance status.

**IoT Security**
Device authentication, encrypted communication, firmware signing, vulnerability management, and compliance with IoT security frameworks.

## End-to-End Capability

We cover the full IoT stack: hardware selection and prototyping, firmware and embedded software, connectivity and protocol design, cloud platform engineering, and the analytics and user-facing applications that make the data actionable.
