## Quality Is Not an Afterthought — It's the Foundation

Releasing broken software is expensive: lost users, damaged reputation, and costly emergency fixes. Our QA engineers are embedded in your development process from day one — not parachuted in at the end to rubber-stamp releases.

## Our Testing Services

**Manual Functional Testing**
End-to-end test execution by experienced QA engineers who think like your users. We explore edge cases, validate business logic, and ensure every feature works as intended across platforms.

**Test Automation**
Robust automated test suites that give you fast, reliable feedback on every code change. We build maintainable automation frameworks — not brittle scripts.

**API Testing**
Comprehensive API test coverage: contract testing, endpoint validation, authentication testing, error handling, and performance under load.

**Performance & Load Testing**
Simulate real-world traffic to identify bottlenecks, memory leaks, and failure thresholds before your users discover them.

**Security Testing**
Identify vulnerabilities before attackers do. We conduct OWASP Top 10 assessments, penetration testing, and security code reviews.

**Mobile Testing**
Functional, compatibility, and performance testing across real iOS and Android devices and screen sizes.

**Accessibility Testing**
WCAG 2.1 compliance testing ensures your software is usable by everyone, including users with disabilities.

## QA Integration Models

- **Embedded QA** — Our QA engineers join your team and participate in every sprint
- **QA Consulting** — We audit your existing processes and build a QA strategy
- **Test Automation Projects** — We build your automation framework and train your team
- **Release Certification** — Independent QA validation before major releases

## What Good QA Delivers

Beyond catching bugs, a mature QA practice means shorter development cycles (bugs found early cost 10x less to fix), greater team confidence in deployments, and a more consistent user experience that builds customer trust.
