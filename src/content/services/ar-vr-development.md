## The Future of Human-Digital Interaction Is Here

AR and VR are moving rapidly from novelty to necessity in industries where spatial understanding, immersive training, or visual product representation provide genuine competitive advantage.

## Our AR/VR Services

**Enterprise Training & Simulation**
Replace costly, risky, or impractical physical training with immersive VR simulations — safety training, medical procedures, manufacturing assembly, and complex skills development.

**Product Visualisation & AR Try-on**
Let customers see products in their real environment before buying. AR overlays for furniture, fashion, eyewear, and industrial equipment reduce returns and increase conversion.

**Virtual Showrooms & Tours**
Immersive virtual showrooms, real estate walkthroughs, and architectural visualisations that let prospects explore spaces and products from anywhere in the world.

**WebAR Experiences**
Browser-based AR that requires no app download — powered by WebXR. Accessible to anyone with a smartphone via a simple URL.

**Mixed Reality for Industry**
HoloLens and MR applications for manufacturing, maintenance, and field service — overlaying critical data and instructions onto real-world equipment.

**Customer Experience & Marketing**
Branded AR filters, interactive campaigns, and immersive brand experiences that create memorable engagement.

## Development Platforms We Work With

- **Mobile AR**: ARKit (iOS), ARCore (Android), Vuforia
- **VR Headsets**: Meta Quest, HTC Vive, Valve Index, PSVR
- **Enterprise MR**: Microsoft HoloLens, Magic Leap
- **Web**: WebXR, A-Frame, Three.js

## Industries We Serve with AR/VR

Healthcare, Manufacturing, Real Estate, Retail, Education, Defence, Architecture, and Tourism — any field where spatial understanding and immersive experience creates measurable business value.
