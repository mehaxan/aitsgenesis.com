## Scale Your Engineering Capacity On Demand

Building a full in-house team is expensive, slow, and risky. Our Dedicated Team model gives you immediate access to battle-tested engineers and specialists who function as a true extension of your organisation — aligned with your culture, processes, and goals.

## How It Works

**Team Composition**
We work with you to define the exact skills and roles you need. From a single senior developer to a complete cross-functional squad, your team is built to specification.

**Onboarding & Integration**
Your dedicated team joins your collaboration tools, attends your standups, follows your coding standards, and commits to your repositories. They're your team — just employed by us.

**Transparent Management**
Full visibility into team activity through daily standups, sprint reviews, and shared project management tools. You always know what's being worked on.

**Flexible Scaling**
Start with a small team, scale up for product launches, scale down during quieter phases. Our model is designed to flex with your business.

## Team Roles Available

- **Software Engineers** — Frontend, backend, and full-stack across all major technology stacks
- **Mobile Developers** — iOS (Swift), Android (Kotlin), and cross-platform (React Native, Flutter)
- **UI/UX Designers** — Product designers, UX researchers, and design system specialists
- **QA Engineers** — Manual testers, automation engineers, and performance testing specialists
- **DevOps Engineers** — Cloud infrastructure, CI/CD, monitoring, and security
- **Scrum Masters / Project Managers** — Agile delivery leadership embedded in your team

## Getting Started

1. **Discovery Call** — We understand your team gaps, technology stack, and working culture.
2. **Team Design** — We propose candidates and team composition.
3. **Interviews** — You interview and approve every team member.
4. **Kickoff** — Onboarding, access provisioning, and sprint planning.
5. **Ongoing Delivery** — Regular delivery with monthly reviews and performance alignment.
