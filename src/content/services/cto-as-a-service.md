## The CTO You Need, When You Need It

Early-stage startups, growing SMEs, and organisations in transition often need experienced technology leadership but aren't ready — or don't have the resources — for a full-time CTO. Our CTO-as-a-Service gives you seasoned executive technology leadership that scales with your needs.

## What a Fractional CTO Does for You

**Technology Strategy**
Translates your business vision into a technology roadmap. Defines the technology bets that drive competitive advantage and the ones to avoid.

**Architecture Oversight**
Provides senior oversight of technical decisions — ensuring the systems being built today won't become the technical debt of tomorrow.

**Engineering Leadership**
Coaches your engineering team, sets quality standards, establishes engineering culture and practices, and helps attract and retain top talent.

**Investor Conversations**
Participates in investor due diligence, board meetings, and fundraising discussions to articulate your technology strategy and team strength with credibility.

**Recruiting & Team Building**
Defines engineering roles, participates in technical interviews, and helps build a high-performing team aligned with your culture and technical direction.

**Vendor & Platform Decisions**
Guides major build-vs-buy decisions, evaluates external vendors, and negotiates technology contracts with your interests protected.

## Engagement Models

**Advisory** (4-8 hours/month)
Strategic guidance, architecture reviews, and monthly leadership meetings.

**Part-time Leadership** (1-2 days/week)
Deep involvement in roadmap planning, team mentoring, and execution oversight.

**Interim CTO** (3-6 months)
Full CTO coverage during a leadership transition, post-funding scale-up, or critical product phase.

## Who Benefits Most?

- **Seed & Series A startups** that need technology leadership alongside a small engineering team
- **Non-technical founders** building technical products who need a trusted technology partner
- **Companies in transition** between technology leaders
- **Enterprises** launching innovation initiatives that need entrepreneurial technology thinking
