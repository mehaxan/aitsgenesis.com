## Technology Decisions That Move the Business Forward

Every technology decision has downstream consequences for years to come. Whether you're re-platforming, selecting a new ERP, designing a cloud architecture, or planning a digital transformation, having an experienced technology advisor changes outcomes.

## Our Consulting Services

**Technology Strategy & Roadmapping**
We work with your leadership to understand business objectives and translate them into a prioritised, actionable technology roadmap that aligns investment with impact.

**IT Architecture Review**
An independent assessment of your current technology architecture — identifying risks, inefficiencies, scalability limitations, and opportunities for modernisation.

**Vendor & Platform Selection**
Objective evaluation of technology platforms and vendors against your specific requirements. We help you avoid costly mistakes and lock-ins.

**Digital Transformation Planning**
End-to-end transformation roadmaps covering process redesign, technology adoption, change management, and success metrics.

**IT Security Assessment**
Comprehensive review of your security posture, identifying vulnerabilities, compliance gaps, and recommendations for a stronger security framework.

**Cloud Migration Strategy**
Detailed migration plans that balance risk, cost, and speed — covering workload assessment, cloud provider selection, and phased migration execution.

**Technology Due Diligence**
For investors and acquirers, we provide in-depth technical assessment of software assets, team capabilities, and technology debt.

## Our Consulting Approach

We don't sell technology — we advise on it. Our consultants have built and run complex systems across industries, which means our recommendations are grounded in real-world implementation experience, not theoretical best practice.

Every engagement starts with listening deeply to understand your business context, constraints, and ambitions — then crafting solutions that are pragmatic, actionable, and measurable.
